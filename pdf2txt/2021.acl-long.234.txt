in this paper, we propose the novel competence-based multimodal curriculum learning framework(cmcl) to alleviate the data bias by efÔ¨Åciently uti-lizing the limited medical data for medical reportgeneration.
to this end, considering the difÔ¨Åcultyof accurately capturing and describing the abnor-malities, we Ô¨Årst assess four sample difÔ¨Åcultiesof training data from the visual complexity andthe textual complexity, resulting in four differentcurricula.
next, cmcl enables the model to betrained with the appropriate curricula and graduallyproceed from easy samples to more complex onesin training.
experimental results demonstrate theeffectiveness and the generalization capabilities ofcmcl, which consistently boosts the performanceof the baselines under most metrics..acknowledgments.
